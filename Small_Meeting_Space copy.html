ABOUT THE SPACE

Everyone wants a desk at Seattle’s finest flexible workspace, Spaces 2 and U.

Description

Spaces 2 and U is located in downtown Seattle's business district – a home it shares with corporate giants including Amazon, as well as top restaurants, bars and cultural attractions such as Seattle Art Museum. This new 38-story building boasts serious curb appeal thanks to its continuous glass façade that provides unobstructed views of the Puget Sound and Olympic Mountains, as well as the buzzing city below. It also means every fully equipped workspace across three floors is filled with natural light – from private offices and chic meeting rooms to designer communal areas perfect for meeting like-minded entrepreneurs or brainstorming your next big idea. These envy-inducing offices even boast a small private deck on the 16th floor as well as a shared deck on the 19th floor, where you can host meetings people will actually look forward to..

Spaces 2 and U is a one-minute walk from multiple bus stops, a block from University Street Station, and benefits from fantastic road links. The building even offers an on-site bike club, storage and service stations. More than simply a place to plug in your laptop, you’ll benefit from an on-site fitness center and yoga studio with a shower and sauna facility. And on the ground floor, there’s always something inspiring to do after a long day thanks to the extensive Urban Village. Offering a creative mix of open seating, lounge areas, local retailers, bars and restaurants, it’s specifically curated to provide an unparalleled retail experience for the tenants and community around the building. The Urban Village also hosts seasonal events – ideal for networking with local professionals.
