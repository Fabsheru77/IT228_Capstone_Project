About the Space Situated on the entire top floor of a boutique building in the heart of South Lake Union, this Penthouse apartment is the envy of Seattle. Wrap around deck and roof deck look toward Lake Union. Although it is an occupied home, the space is perfect for hosting business meetings and executive retreats. Please note that I do not host social events. Nearly 1800 square feet (167 meters square) of interior space plus a wrap-around deck, the 2 bedroom, 2 bath Penthouse designed by Graham Baba is a work of art. Polished concrete floors throughout, white oak cabinetry and built-ins, blacked steel wall accents, exposed steel structure, natural fir ceiling and exquisite bath and kitchen fixtures express a wholly Northwest material palette. WiFi throughout serving Lumen 1GB internet speeds, 4k TV with Apple TV and Samsung wireless connections. 3x5 rolling white board and projector for use in kitchen/dining with HDMI connection..
